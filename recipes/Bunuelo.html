Bunuelos

Description

The Bunuelo recipe is a typical dessert in many autonomous communities and, above all, during their regional festivals. Each territory incorporates its own ingredients and its own tradition.

One of the best known in Nicaragua country is the Yucca Bunuelos, this recipe has a delicious taste and it is very popular in some of the Nicaragua departments, for example, Carazo, Masaya, and Granada.

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds of yucca
  • 1/2 pound brown sugar
  • 1 pound dry cheese
  • 1/2 liter oil
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 2 Cinnamon sticks

Instructions:

  1. Wash the yucca and peel it, then split it to grate it along with the cheese.
  2. Then add the baking powder and leave it at rest for about 10 to 12 minutes
  3. Once the time is over, start forming the balls.
  4. We light our fire and pour oil into our casserole and when it is very hot we fry them.
  5. The idea is that the balls are submerged so that the bark is fried to perfection.
  6. Once they look golden, we take out with a spoon, drain the oil and then serve it.
